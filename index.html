Fensterchef

Fensterchef is an organisation writing free software for free unix systems.
We versatile the free software market with powerful and performant software.

This website is currently dedicated to our only product: The X11 window manager.

Fensterchef is a flexible and lightweight tiling window manager for X11. It offers high performance while maintaining high configurability and extensibility.

Wayland

When X11 is pushed out by Wayland completely, we will be ready with Wayland replacements.
